WU CHENG-RU

New Media Artist  ·  Creative Coder  ·  AV Performer

Born in Chiayi, Taiwan, a new media artist whose work primarily involves interdisciplinary art and audiovisual performances. His practice centers on sound experimentation, real-time image generation, and digital signal integration. His work focuses on human relationships, exploring the cognitive differences in mutual understanding through distortions in digital signal transmission.

He has been actively engaged in a wide range of creative fields, including interdisciplinary art, theater, dance, and sound installations, continually expanding the innovative possibilities of new media art.

來自嘉義，科技藝術創作者，創作探討各種媒材之間的關係美學，透過訊號失真的傳遞，思考關係中的認知差異。作品跨足音像演出、聲音裝置、科技劇場，由各種媒材中探索創作上的可能性。

曾參與白晝之夜作品《鹹淡適中》Glub Glub、C-Lab Dome 計畫《未來視覺派對 FUTURE VISION LAB》、關渡光藝術節，等作品展演計畫。
